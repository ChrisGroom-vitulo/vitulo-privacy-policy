Privacy Policy for Vitulo Health

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Vitulo Health ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or use Vitulo Health, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Role within your organization (farm staff or administrator)
  • Authentication credentials (PIN or password, stored securely)
  • Farm/site assignment information

1.2 Livestock Data

To provide livestock management services, we collect:

  • Animal identification (ear tags, electronic IDs)
  • Health records and treatment history
  • Medication and protocol information
  • Weight measurements and growth data
  • Mortality and health event records
  • Movement and location history within your farm

1.3 Device and Usage Information

  • Bluetooth Data: We access Bluetooth to connect to weighing scales and EID wands for data collection
  • Location Data: Used only to discover nearby Bluetooth devices (not for tracking your location)
  • Camera and Photos: Optional access for documenting health issues and problem reports
  • Device Information: Operating system version, device model (for technical support)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide livestock management services and functionality
  • Enable offline data recording and synchronization
  • Generate performance reports and analytics
  • Facilitate communication between farm staff and management
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure data accuracy and system security

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II compliant cloud infrastructure provider.

3.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the app and servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored on your device is encrypted using industry-standard encryption (MMKV)
  • Authentication: Secure login with rate limiting to prevent unauthorized access
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions ensure users only access authorized data
  • Regular Backups: Automated backups protect against data loss

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within Your Organization

Your livestock data is shared with authorized users within your farm organization (staff, administrators) for operational purposes.

4.2 We Do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share data with advertisers or marketers
  • ❌ Use your data for purposes unrelated to livestock management
  • ❌ Access your photos without your explicit consent

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

5. Offline Data and Synchronization

Vitulo Health supports offline functionality:

  • Data recorded offline is stored securely on your device
  • Automatic synchronization occurs when internet connection is restored
  • Offline data is encrypted using the same security standards
  • You can review offline data before it syncs

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Required to provide services
  • Necessary for legal, regulatory, or business requirements

Upon account deletion, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Vitulo Health is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in data centers located in various countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications
  • Updated "Last Updated" date on this page

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure (SOC 2 compliant)
  • Hardware Integration: Connection to Tru-Test, Allflex, and other livestock equipment manufacturers

These services have their own privacy policies and security standards.
